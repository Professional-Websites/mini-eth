In general, bots are only interested in the birth of coins, they don’t pay much attention to a coin after its released into the world. The initial investors into our coins will be real people, rather than bots constantly trying and suck the life out of your investment.

FAIR LAUNCHES

As well as our launches being stealth, they will also be fair. The moment we launch the coin into the world we will publish the contract address on our website and announce it in our telegram group, giving everyone the exact same chance to buy.

LOCKED LIQUIDITY

Before we announce a launch liquidity will be locked till 2100 with Dx Locker. A link to the locked liquidity will be published at the same time as the contract address. This ensures that its impossible for us to rug pull the liquidity of the coin from under you.

NO DEV TOKENS

100% of all the tokens generated will be locked into the initial liquidity. This will mean that there will be no dumping on the chart by a greedy development team. There will be no minting function in the contract, which means no more tokens can be generated after the initial supply has been created.

ANTI-SCAM

This stops scammers. All you need to do to avoid getting scammed is NEVER buy any token from a contract address that has not been published on our website. (I do hope people bother to read this!!!)

ANTI-WHALE

No wallet address will be able to hold more than 2% of the supply. This massively reduces the chance of whales controlling the supply of the coin and reduces the chance of large dumps on the chart
